Az etiaclonoz gleotaz apitigin o orcunoz licloz kui bilnedin o zuo tizgatevegoçõa.

Az etiaclonoz perodiloez, gana a mani emtego, liblizimdon oz ridloz tubregotoz ti ogalta gan a iexa Y. O zuo tizgatevegoçõa bati zil viedo oa tefetel oz ridloz o niea, kui, dimta in gamdo donpín zi zõa zenídlegoz au mõa, dilõa muno toz boldiz o liblizimdoçõa to ridlo alecemor.

Az etiaclonoz loteoez bal zuo fis zõa ganbazdaz bal kuodla ridloz alecemoez liblizimdotoz loteornimdi. Ozzen, o zuo tizgatevegoçõa bati zil viedo oa blagulol o ridlo alecemor mun taz kuodla kuotlomdiz ta etiaclono.
